Floating Shelf

Floating Shelf | APRIL 25, 2018

The fake-plant trend is still going strong in the world of interior design. Finding the right space to place these items can be difficult; especially when a space is almost filled up. We want a room to feel nice and open rather than cluttered and restricted. With this easy-to-make floating shelf, you can transform your living space and satisfy your plant-décor cravings.

For this tutorial, you will need:

  • Round wooden plate from Home Depot
  • Scissors
  • Yarn

Source: This DIY came from Place of My Taste.

STEP 1:

Cut about 48, 3.5 yards pieces of yarn. Then, tie a knot in their center. It’s going to be the centerpiece of the hanger.

STEP 2:

Divide your yarn into four equal sections of 12. Then divide each part into two sections of 6 and twist them together. You can also separate your sections to three and braid them.

STEP 3:

Hang your piece someplace high. Tie a knot approximately 4 inches below the top. As the last step tie a knot at the bottom. Finally, slip on your wooden plate.
